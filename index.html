WHAT IS BOLUNTARYONG TUPIANS

Boluntaryong TUPians is an organization that promotes uprightness, is responsible for answering queries, and presenting clear responses.

BOLUNTARYO is derived from voluntarism, a Tagalog word meaning referring to voluntary acts that are not forced or compelled in serving students.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide students with consistent, rightful yet authentic answers on their inquiries for the Boluntaryong TUPians legitimate contacts.

To render students with an avenue opportunity to query and speak their concerns, sentiments, and suggestions to convey development and transformation within the system.

To give students an opportunity to expand their skills and abilities by providing them with collaborative activities as a space to enhance their talents and expertise.

Vision

We aim for aspiring TUPians who genuinely love the University, to find space and comfort in maximizing their skills and abilities, and manifest potential in leading the TUP to a well-organized and a career-oriented community.

As the Student-Volunteers, the Boluntaryong TUPians, are unwaveringly improving themselves to passionately serve, and assist the students better.
